About Me

I am a fourth-year Echols Scholar at the University of Virginia. I am now a member of UVA-MLSys where I work with Professor Judy Fox for the "AI For Science" project and an undergarduate researcher at the Information and Language Processing (ILP) Lab at UVA led by Professor Yangfeng Ji. I have a particular interest in applying computational methods empowered by modern CS developments into the study of social science. After graduation, I plan to pursue a Master Degree in Computer Science or in related fields. I speak English, Chinese, and French.
